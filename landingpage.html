WELCOME TO KAPAYAPAAN SCHOOL

Kapayapaan School is a nurturing and inclusive learning community that values academic excellence, character development, and a peaceful environment. It aims to help students grow with confidence, creativity, and compassion.
Everyone—students, parents, and visitors—is warmly welcomed to be part of a
family that inspires learning, leadership, and purpose

ABOUT OF THE SCHOOL

Kapayapaan School is a values-centered learning institution that promotes holistic education and student development.

It focuses not only on academic excellence but also on building character, leadership, and social responsibility.

The school provides a safe and inclusive environment where students are encouraged to grow,

explore their talents, and become future leaders with integrity and compassion

EVENT OF THE SCHOOL

Kapayapaan School hosts a variety of meaningful events throughout the year that support both academic and personal growth.
These include foundation day celebrations, educational fairs, sports festivals, and cultural showcases that highlight student talents and teamwork.

The school also conducts outreach programs and leadership seminars to encourage social awareness and community involvement.
These events aim to enrich students’ experiences and foster a strong sense of unity, pride, and purpose within the school community.

MISSION

Kapayapaan School is committed to providing quality and holistic education that nurtures academic excellence, moral integrity, and social responsibility.

We aim to develop well-rounded individuals who are prepared to face the challenges of the future with confidence, compassion, and competence

Vision

We envision Kapayapaan School as a center of learning that fosters peace, discipline, and excellence—producing globally competitive and values-driven graduates who contribute positively to society.

We aim to nurture individuals who are not only academically competent but also morally grounded and socially responsible.
